About Me

I'm adventurous. I like to learn and experience new things. I've been an Intensive Care Unit nurse for the last ten years, and I've been the creative executive for Intrinsic Value Films, an indie film production company for the last five, where I work in screenplay development. I've published numerous poetry and prose pieces in literary journals and anthologies.

My newest adventure is software development. I completed a 12 week bootcamp for Front End Engineering through The Iron Yard. I am now able to create applications that interweave health care and storytelling. I am now able to write interactive prose and poetry pieces. I now have the possiblity to touch many more lives than the few hundreds of patients I've cared for as a critical care nurse. Some people are either right side or left side brains. I'm both.
